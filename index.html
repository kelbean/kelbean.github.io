WHAT IS PERSONA?

Persona is a critically acclaimed role-playing game (RPG) series developed by Atlus, known for its unique blend of traditional turn-based combat, deep character development, and social simulation.

STORY & OVERVIEW

A spin-off of the Shin Megami Tensei franchise, Persona follows high school students who lead double livesâ€”balancing everyday life, friendships, and school while secretly battling supernatural threats using manifestations of their inner selves called "Personas."

Each game explores psychological and philosophical themes such as identity, rebellion, and the duality of human nature, set against stylish visuals, memorable soundtracks, and modern urban settings. Popular titles like Persona 3, Persona 4, and Persona 5 have earned global praise for pushing the boundaries of storytelling in video games.
